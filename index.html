Why a Web App?

Many of our brothers and sisters face limited access to high-speed internet or mobile app stores. A web app ensures universal accessibility:

  • You can access it through your browser without needing a download.
  • Save it to your device by selecting "Add to Home Screen" in your browser.
  • It works efficiently even with minimal internet access.

Our web app bridges the gap between accessibility and efficiency, making it ideal for anyone, anywhere.
